# Operations Readiness

This checklist evaluates operational readiness for launch.

## Ownership

### Team Responsibilities

* Technical owner assigned
* Operations owner assigned
* Support owner assigned

## Incident Response

### Preparation

* Incident response plan documented
* Escalation matrix defined
* Severity levels defined

### Communication

* Status update process documented
* Community communication plan prepared

## Support Readiness

### Customer Support

* Support channels active
* FAQ prepared
* Troubleshooting guides available

## Business Continuity

### Recovery

* Backup procedures documented
* Service restoration process documented

## Launch Day Operations

### Go Live Checklist

* Team availability confirmed
* Monitoring dashboard reviewed
* Support coverage assigned

## Post Launch

### Review Process

* Success metrics defined
* Incident review process documented
* Post-mortem process prepared
